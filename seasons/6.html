Lineup Restrictions

  • Region Lock: No region can appear in more than one deck.
  • Collection Lock: The lineup can’t include more than three copies of each card total.
  • Riot Lock: Unique Champions and Unique Region Combinations with open decklists.

Match Format

  • Conquest: Each game, each player uses any unbanned deck in their lineup that hasn’t won a game that match.
