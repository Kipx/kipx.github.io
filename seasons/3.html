Lineup Restrictions

  • Unique Regions: No region can appear in more than one deck.
  • Unique Region Combinations: No region combination can be used more than once.
  • Unique Cards: No card can appear in more than one deck.
  • Linked Cards: The lineup can’t include more than three copies of each card total.
  • Triangulated Regions with Unique Champions: Pick three regions and make three decks with the three unique region combinations of those three regions, and you cannot use the same champion between decks.

Match Format

  • Conquest: Each game, each player uses any unbanned deck in their lineup that hasn’t won a game that match.
